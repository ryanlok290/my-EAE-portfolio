-- My Origin

How It All Started

The moment cybersecurity stopped being "just a subject" and became something I couldn't stop thinking about.

It started from Primary School.

I have a strong interest in coding and technology. My interest was piqued during primary school when I joined the Robotics CCA, where I explored the basics of Robotics and learnt how to code. I have continued to develop my skills through attending courses in coding and AI during school holidays. I enjoy the logical thinking behind coding and the way it allows ideas to become something real.

That curiosity turned into late nights readings on Technology news articles, then into exploring website creation, then into signing up for online courses related to AI and Cybersecurity during my school breaks.

What kept me hooked was not the idea of "breaking in", but the puzzle of thinking one step ahead of whoever's trying to get in, and building something that holds. That's the version of cybersecurity I want to keep getting better at: less about knowing every tool, more about learning to think like both the attacker and the defender at once.

Eventually, I aspire to pursue Cybersecurity in the future, with the long-term goal of becoming a Cybersecurity professional and leader.
